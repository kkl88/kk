Oak Tree

Flora entry — field observation

← Back to Flora

Observation Notes

Observed a mature oak tree near the trail edge. Leaves are broad and starting to turn yellow in late summer. Bark is rough with deep grooves. Noted presence of acorns on the ground.

Additional Notes

  • Height approx. 12 meters
  • Surrounding plants: ferns, wild grasses
  • Animal activity: birds perching, squirrels feeding on acorns
